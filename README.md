\## ğŸ“– About



\*\*HostelCare AI\*\* is an innovative voice-based complaint management system designed to empower visually impaired hostel students. Using the Web Speech API, students can voice their complaints, which are converted to text and stored for review by the warden.



This accessible and user-friendly solution promotes inclusivity and dignity for students with visual impairments, addressing a critical gap in existing hostel management systems. By providing a seamless and efficient way to report issues, HostelCare AI enhances the overall hostel living experience, ensuring that all students can voice their concerns and receive timely support.
